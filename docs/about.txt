[ Fango.Money ]

PRIVATE TRANSACTIONS, DECENTRALIZED BANKING, & SECURE MESSAGING

Fango is a decentralized blockchain bank with on-ledger deposits paying interest, without involvement of financial institutions, powered by 100% open source code.

Fango network enables secure untraceable transfer of funds and anonymous messaging using a distributed public ledger, the sender and receiver are kept anonymous, a key concern in a post Snowden world.
Hackers cannot trace money or messages when the messages are sent across public networks.

Fango (XFG) is based on the Cryptonote protocol and runs on a secure peer-to-peer network technology to operate with no central authority. You control the private keys to your funds.

No one owns Fango network and everyone can take part.  Fango is open-source, community driven and truly decentralized - accessible by anyone in the world regardless of their geographic location or status.
Fango blockchain is resistant to any kind of analysis.  All your transactions and messages are anonymous.
